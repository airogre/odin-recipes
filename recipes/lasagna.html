Lasagna Recipe


Description

This recipe has been passed down from generation to generation in my family. Guests will love it!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Pasta
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Pasta Sauce

Steps

  1. Step 1: Preheat oven to 425 degrees
  2. Step 2: While oven is preheating, layer lasagna
  3. Step 3: Bake in oven for 25 minutes
