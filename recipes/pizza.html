Pizza Recipe


Description

This pizza is both easy to cook and delicious!

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Mozzerella Cheese
  • Pasta Sauce

Steps

  1. Step 1: Preheat oven to 425 degrees
  2. Step 2: While oven is preheating, spread sauce and then sprinkle cheese onto pizza dough
  3. Step 3: Bake in oven for 15 minutes
