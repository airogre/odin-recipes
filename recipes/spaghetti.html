Spaghetti Recipe


Description

This quick dish is great for a busy weeknight.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti Pasta
  • Pasta Sauce
  • 2 Quarts Water

Steps

  1. Step 1: Heat water in pot
  2. Step 2: When water is boiling, put spaghetti in and cook for 8-9 minutes
  3. Step 3: Drain cooked spaghetti
  4. Step 4: Stir in pasta sauce
